Swimming Tips

  • Hydrate before and after swimming
  • Warm up before entering pool

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about swimming fitness and routines

What dryland exercises help swimmers most?

Core, shoulder stability, and leg strength exercises are most effective for swimmers.

How often should I do mobility work?

2-3 times a week is ideal for most swimmers to maintain healthy joints and prevent injuries.

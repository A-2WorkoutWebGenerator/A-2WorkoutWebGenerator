Physiotherapy Information & Training Routines

Physiotherapy (also known as physical therapy) is a healthcare profession focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of physical impairments, movement dysfunctions, and disabilities. It helps restore function, improve mobility, relieve pain, and prevent further injury.

Football Wellness Tips

  • Always warm-up before playing
  • Hydrate before, during, and after matches
  • Use proper footwear for your playing surface
  • Focus on technique to avoid injury
  • Include strength & flexibility routines

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about football fitness and routines

How often should I train football-specific strength?

2-3 sessions per week are ideal for maintaining and building football-specific strength, especially during the off-season.

What recovery is best after a match?

Cooldown exercises, stretching, hydration, and light foam rolling help reduce soreness and speed recovery after matches.

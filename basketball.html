Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about basketball fitness and routines

How can I jump higher for basketball?

Plyometric exercises and strength training for the legs and core are key to increasing vertical jump.

What is the best way to prevent basketball injuries?

Proper warm-up, cool-down, strength and flexibility routines, and learning safe landing mechanics are essential for injury prevention.

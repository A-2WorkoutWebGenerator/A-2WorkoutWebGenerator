Kinetotherapy Overview & Exercise Routines

Kinetotherapy is a therapeutic discipline based on human movement. It is used to improve strength, flexibility, coordination, and function through active and passive exercises. Kinetotherapy is commonly applied in rehabilitation after injuries, surgeries, or neurological disorders.

Recommended Kinetotherapy Exercise Routines

1. Postural Correction Routine

  • Wall posture alignment
  • Scapular retraction exercises
  • Chin tucks
  • Thoracic spine extensions

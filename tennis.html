Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about tennis fitness and routines

How can I prevent tennis elbow?

Strengthen your forearm muscles, use proper technique, and avoid overtraining to minimize tennis elbow risk.

How often should I train for tennis?

2-4 times per week, including both on-court play and off-court conditioning, is ideal for most players.
